Why Choose TRIDNA

Your Mission, Our Metrics – Turning Data into Decisions that Drive Change. An overview of TRIDNA’s data and analytics consulting service for governments, non-profit organizations, and philanthropic partners.

Who We Serve:

TRIDNA is the premier partner for public sector partners to improve their understanding of how data impacts their operations and outcomes. At the core of our consulting practice, we help mission-driven, public-focused organizations unlock insights, measure impact, and make smarter choices with improved usage of organization data. We specialize in data strategy, analytics, and visualization for nonprofits and social enterprises. From dashboards to predictive modeling, we turn complex information into clear, actionable stories.

How We can Help:

Data today is key for every industry… especially those organizations focused on improving society. Most important, we know that data clarity equates to improved effectiveness. Our tools and expertise help you focus on what matters—we make your data actionable for you to amplify your mission, better engage stakeholders, and drive measurable outcomes. Our process is to get to know your purpose and desired results, review your current data collection methods and analytical strategies, and then determine how success in both of these areas can be better achieved with new expertise and insight. As a company, our goal is to align ourselves primarily with organizations with a public focus because we know that this is where data and analytics can make the most impact, but also know that this is where it is least used to improved outcomes.

About Us

TRIDNA is more than just a company that designs and implements applications in various industries and performs data and analytics for clients. Our executive team believes in a corporate culture that is committed to doing great work and being a great citizen. You can see this theme in the apps that we develop which are aimed toward helping those without a lot of resources or access to improve their situation. This is also identified in our commitment to providing our expertise to government and non-profit organizations to be more successful in their operations for public improvement.
